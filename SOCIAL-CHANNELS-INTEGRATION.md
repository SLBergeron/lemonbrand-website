# Social Channels Integration Guide

**Purpose:** Complete reference for integrating Simon's social channels into LemonBrand.io
**Date:** November 13, 2025
**Tone:** Raw, honest, real - no fake guru shit

---

## Core Philosophy

**In a world full of fake, I'm something real.**

Everything I post is:
- Raw and unedited (or minimally edited)
- Honest about wins AND failures
- Showing the actual work, not just talking about it
- Building in public, documenting the messy middle

I don't do:
- Fake thumbnails with arrows and shocked faces
- Clickbait titles that don't match content
- Polished "personal brand" bullshit
- Guru energy or fake success

## Copy Guidelines

### Voice & Tone

**Do:**
- "Raw build sessions"
- "Honest thoughts"
- "Real work"
- "Mistakes and all"
- "Figuring it out"
- "What's actually happening"
- "Documenting everything"

**Don't:**
- "Professional tutorials"
- "Expert insights"
- "Proven strategies"
- "Subscribe for more awesome content"
- "Hit the bell icon"
- "Smash that like button"
- Any guru/influencer speak

### Social CTAs

**YouTube:**
- "Subscribe on YouTube" (not "Subscribe for more!")
- "Watch on YouTube" (not "Check out my channel!")
- "See the full build" (not "Full tutorial here!")

**TikTok:**
- "Follow @simonbergeron" (not "Follow for daily tips!")
- "Watch on TikTok" (not "More content on TikTok!")

**X/Twitter:**
- "Follow @SLBergeron" (not "Follow for updates!")
- "See on X" (not "Tweet at me!")

**Newsletter:**
- "Join the newsletter" or "Subscribe" (not "Sign up for exclusive content!")
- "Get weekly updates" (not "Never miss an update!")

**GitHub:**
- "View on GitHub" (not "Star my repos!")
- "See the code" (not "Check out my projects!")

## Design Guidelines

### Icons
- Use simple, outline-style icons (not filled)
- Consistent size across all social links
- Orange/yellow accent on hover (matches brand)

### Layout
- Social links always in order: YouTube â†’ TikTok â†’ X â†’ Newsletter â†’ GitHub
- Desktop: Horizontal or grid layout
- Mobile: Vertical stack

### Colors
- Default: Neutral gray
- Hover: Orange gradient (matches CTA buttons)
- Active: Orange solid

### Spacing
- Generous padding around social sections
- Clear visual separation from other content
- Social links should feel integrated, not tacked on

## Video Embed Strategy

### YouTube Video Embeds

**Where to embed:**
- Homepage: Latest 3 videos
- Projects page: Relevant build videos per project
- Templates page: Template usage videos (when available)

**How to embed:**
```jsx
// Option 1: YouTube iframe (simple)
<iframe
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID"
  title="Video title"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowFullScreen
/>

// Option 2: Custom video component with thumbnail
<VideoEmbed
  videoId="VIDEO_ID"
  thumbnail="/path/to/thumbnail.jpg"
  title="Video title"
  views={287}
  published="2 days ago"
/>
```

**Design:**
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Rounded corners (matches site design)
- Border on hover
- Play button overlay on thumbnail

## The Bottom Line

**Every social link should:**
1. Be honest about what people will find
2. Match your "raw and real" brand
3. Not feel like desperate "follow me" energy
4. Show you're actively building and sharing
5. Make it easy to follow in their preferred format

**The goal isn't virality. The goal is documentation.**

People who vibe with your raw, honest approach will follow.
People who want polished guru content won't.

That's the filter. That's the point.
